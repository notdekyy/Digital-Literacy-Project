# Social Media Responsibility Checklist

## The Do's (Best Practices)

* Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal content & information.
* Think twice before posting -- make sure that what you are posting represents the kind of professional image you want future employers to see of you.
* Verify the content you are posting with other credible sources of information to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Engage in respectful & constructive dialogue with others on Social Media even if you disagree with them.
* Use Social Media to show off your skills, projects, & academic achievements.

## The Don'ts (Common Pitfalls)

* **Never** post any content that is offensive, discriminatory, hateful & goes against university policies.
* **Never** share any of your personal info like home address, phone number, etc. 
* **Never** engage in cyber bullying, harassment & arguments with others on Social Media.
* **Never** post pictures & videos of illegal or unprofessional behavior.
* **Never** plagiarize -- claiming someone else's work as your own.
