# What I Created:
In order to fulfil my role as a Student Digital Ambassador, I used Canva to produce a professional-level infographic with the title **‘Mastering Digital Literacy: Tools, Safety & Presence.’** My infographic covers three key topics: 

* Useful Digital Tools for Students.

* Safe Internet Practices.

* Professional Online Presence.

# Design Description:
My design is strategically divided into three major areas that serve as a visual guide for my fellow batchmates. The key elements include:

* The first section, ‘Useful Digital Tools for Students,’ utilizes simple iconography to show how digital media can be used in the organization, concentration and effective use of time.

* The middle section focuses on ‘Safe Internet Practices,’ which urges the students to use good passwords, identify phishing and protect their data.

* Lastly, the ‘Professional Online Presence’ section encourages my batchmates to thoughtfully manage their online footprint, which includes sites such as LinkedIn.
